The shown curve represents the hourly generation of the highest generation day for your location. Compare it with the data of your monitoring app.

If the curves are different you may have one of the following issues:

  • Close shading
  • Far shading (e. g. mountains)
  • Soiling
  • Modules broken/disconnected
  • Overheating
  • Snow
  • Inverter failure
